This tool allows you to generate a craftingrecipe for any craftable colour of leather armor in Minecraft(Java Edition)


or


The rgb-value of the 16 basic colours available changed two times since the introduction of dyeable leather armor (introduction in 1.4.2, changes in 1.4.3 and 1.12).

There is a total of 5 713 438 craftable colours in versions 1.12 and above
There is a total of 4 200 779 craftable colours in versions 1.4.3 - 1.11.2
There is a total of 4 271 547 craftable colours in version 1.4.2
The rgb colour space contains 256^3 = 16 777 216 total colours
